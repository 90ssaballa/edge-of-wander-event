THE ARTISTS

Joana Carvalhas

The Tiny Musician

Joana Carvalhas is a multi-style musician actively performing and teaching in Europe, the United States of America and the United Kingdom. She graduated from the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland (UK). She plays jazz, free improvisation, and dynamic concerts with live looping and special effects.

She has performed across the world and tours often in the US, UK, and different countries in Europe. Recently, she shared the stage with Lindsey Stirling in Madrid, Spain.

Serg Saballa

Serg Saballa, IFGA is an arthouse filmmaker whose collaboration spans from Hollywood actors to independent artists. His works are internationally recognized and featured in several highly curated film festivals. When not in the directors chair he’s out experimenting with darkgaze music, prototyping spacecrafts, runs a boutique fund and a healthcare tech.
